OUR SPECIALISED PROGRAMMES

Tailored interventions designed to meet each child's unique needs

AUTISM

AUTISM LEARNING & BEHAVIOURAL SUPPORT

  • Communication (PECS, visuals, expressive speech)
  • Behaviour (Emotional control, transitions)
  • Sensory (Sensory integration & motor planning)
  • Daily Routine (Toileting, feeding, dressing)
  • Social Interaction (Eye contact, turn-taking, teamwork)

APPROACHES USED:

Visual structure (TEACCH), Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA), DIR/Floor time, Social stories & play-based learning

DYSLEXIA

DYSLEXIA & LEARNING SUPPORT PROGRAMME

  • Phonemic Awareness & Phonics
  • Reading Fluency & Comprehension
  • Spelling Patterns
  • Structured Writing
  • Confidence in Learning

APPROACHES USED:

Multisensory phonics, Visual aids, Word families, Games, One-on-one & Small group sessions

NEURO DEVELOPMENT

NEURODEVELOPMENTAL SUPPORT (ADHD, GDD, ID)

  • Impulsivity & Focus
  • Academics/Vocational Skills
  • Self-Care & Daily Living Skills
  • Social-Emotional Learning
  • Executive Functioning

APPROACHES USED:

Behavioural coaching, Visual task schedules, Social-emotional games, Movement-based learning

PROGRAMME TRACKS

Age-appropriate learning paths for every developmental stage

AUTISM TRACKS

  • Ages 4-8: Learn through play and routine
  • Ages 9-12: Build skills for school success
  • Ages 13-17: Prepare for life and work

DYSLEXIA TRACKS

  • Ages 6-11: Literacy Booster
  • Ages 12-15: Remedial Academic

LITERACY BOOSTER (Ages 6-11):

Learn to read using phonics and fun activities, Practice writing and spelling step-by-step, Improve vocabulary and understanding of stories, Build confidence in using language every day

REMEDIAL ACADEMIC (Ages 12-15):

Strengthen core subjects in small groups, Learn at their own pace, Get help with schoolwork and exams, Build focus, motivation, and confidence

NEURO DEVELOPMENT TRACKS

  • Ages 4-8: Learn through play and structure
  • Ages 9-12: Strengthen academic and social foundations
  • Ages 13-17: Prepare for independence and future
